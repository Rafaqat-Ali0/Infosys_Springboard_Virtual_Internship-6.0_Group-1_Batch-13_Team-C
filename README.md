# FoodTrends: Understanding Customer Preferences in Food & Beverage

---

## Project Overview
This project was completed as part of the Infosys Springboard Virtual Internship.

FoodTrends is a data visualization project developed using Power BI to analyze customer preferences in the food and beverage domain. It focuses on identifying patterns in dietary habits, nutrient intake, and lifestyle choices to understand how these factors influence customer behavior and health outcomes.

The project transforms raw data into interactive dashboards, enabling better insights into consumption trends, nutritional balance, and lifestyle impact.

## Key Features

Interactive Dashboards:  
Visual representation of customer dietary preferences and consumption patterns.

Nutrients & Health Analysis:  
Identifies relationships between nutrient intake and potential health issues.

Breakfast Behavior Analysis:  
Compares different breakfast choices and their nutritional value.

Lifestyle Impact Insights:  
Analyzes how lifestyle habits influence nutrition and overall well-being.

KPI Metrics:  
Tracks key indicators such as average intake, dietary distribution, and lifestyle influence.
